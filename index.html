Who this is for

This is for teams running large, repeatable AI workloads where cost matters more than latency.

You process large batches (documents, embeddings, classification)
Jobs run in the background and don’t need realtime responses
You can split work into smaller chunks and retry when needed
You care about cost per result more than speed
You occasionally reprocess data (re-indexing, backfills, corrections)
You need low-latency APIs or synchronous user-facing calls
Every job must succeed in a single run with no retries
You need strict SLAs or guaranteed completion times
Built for retry-safe workloads, where occasional reprocessing is acceptable and the focus is on efficiency, reliability, and predictable execution.
