Privacy

Plain privacy notes for website visitors and pilot inquiries.

Last updated: 13 May 2026. This page covers the public Solvyr website, email contact, and early Fast Corpus pilot scoping.

Contact

Who is responsible

Solvyr OÜ, an Estonian limited company with registry code 17471643, is responsible for the personal data it receives through website visits, email inquiries, and pilot scoping conversations.

  • Registered address: Sepapaja tn 6, 15551 Tallinn, Harju Maakond, Estonia.
  • Privacy contact: privacy@solvyr.com.

Boundary

Do not send sensitive data first

Do not send sensitive, regulated, confidential, or production source documents through public email or the intake page before there is a written agreement and an agreed transfer channel.

Data collected

What Solvyr may process

  • Contact details you provide, such as name, organization, and email address.
  • Workload details you choose to share, such as document counts, input type, output requirements, data sensitivity, and timeline.
  • Email metadata and message content when you contact Solvyr.
  • Ordinary technical access logs that may be created by website hosting or security infrastructure.

Purpose

Why it is used

  • To reply to inquiries and assess whether a Fast Corpus pilot is a fit.
  • To prepare a scoped pilot, quote, security discussion, or written agreement.
  • To operate and protect the website and prevent misuse.
  • To keep necessary business, contracting, and legal records.

Sharing

Who may receive it

Solvyr does not sell visitor or inquiry data. Data may be processed by service providers needed for email, hosting, security, administration, or agreed pilot delivery. Pilot subprocessors and handling details should be covered in the written pilot agreement before sensitive or production data is shared.

Retention

How long it is kept

Inquiry and scoping records are kept only as long as needed to respond, assess fit, manage a possible business relationship, or meet legal and administrative obligations. You can ask Solvyr to delete inquiry data unless it must be kept for a legal, contractual, or security reason.

Cookies

No intentional tracking cookies

The public Solvyr website is designed as a static site and does not intentionally set analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or tracking pixels. If analytics or tracking is added later, this page and the cookie approach should be updated before it goes live.

Rights

Your privacy rights

You may ask for access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or object to certain processing by emailing privacy@solvyr.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority. Solvyr does not use website or intake data for automated decision-making.
