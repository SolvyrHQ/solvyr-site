How it works (high level)

Work-unit model

Jobs are broken into small, bounded tasks (seconds to a few minutes). Failures are expected. Retries are first-class.

Unreliable nodes, reliable outcomes

Nodes pull work. Tasks are reassigned when needed. Correctness and repeatability matter more than raw speed.

Deliberately constrained

Early stage. Limited workload classes. No self-serve UI. We prioritize learning and survivability over feature breadth.
